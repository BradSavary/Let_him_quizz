How To Play ?

Ticking away

Get ready for a fast-paced challenge! You’ll have 30 seconds to answer as many questions as possible.

The catch? They come at you quickly, so you need to click on the correct answers faster than a speeding bullet!
Keep your wits sharp and see how many you can get right before time runs out.

Ready, set, go!

Find the place

Get ready for a mind-bending visual challenge!

A 360° picture will appear around you, and your job is to guess exactly where you are.

With 3 rounds to test your knowledge, it's time to see how well you know the world around you!

Don’t worry if you get lost — it's all part of the fun!

Sort it out

Think carefully!

Four boxes and four cubes will appear in front of you, and a question will pop up.

Your task? Sort the cubes in the correct order by placing them in the right boxes.

Once you think everything is in place, hit the button to validate your answer.

No rush, but be sure to double-check before you confirm!

sorry not so rich

Now it’s time to test your strategy!

A question will appear, and you’ll have to decide how much of the money you’ve earned in previous rounds you want to risk.

Drop your cash onto the correct trap based on your answer, but be careful if you’re wrong, you lose everything you bet!

Will you play it safe or take a chance for a bigger prize?
